Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our wedding venue services in Singapore

How far in advance should we book the wedding venue?

We recommend booking your venue at least 3–6 months in advance, especially during peak wedding seasons in Singapore. This ensures your preferred date and hall are available.

What is included in the venue package?

Our wedding packages include venue rental, stage setup, lighting, basic décor, seating arrangements, and air-conditioning. Optional add-ons like catering, photography, and entertainment are also available.

Do you provide catering services or can we bring our own caterer?

Yes, we offer in-house catering featuring both local Singaporean and international cuisines. If you prefer an external caterer, we’re happy to coordinate with them to ensure smooth service and setup.

Is parking available for guests?

Yes, we provide ample on-site parking for guests, with valet options available upon request. For larger gatherings, we can assist in arranging nearby overflow parking.
